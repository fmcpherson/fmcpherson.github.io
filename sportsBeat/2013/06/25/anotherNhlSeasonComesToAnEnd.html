Another NHL Season Comes To An End

The horrific 17 seconds (for Bruins fans) during the final minutes of game six of the 2013 Stanley Cup finals leave me with an empty feeling. A final as good as this one has been deserves a game seven, and I am a bit sad to know that the games are over. After all the battles and overtimes it just doesn't feel right that the series comes to an end on a somewhat flukey goal.

I also dispute the select of Kane for the Conne Smythe trophy for the playoff's MVP. Kane has been good, but frankly, Crawford was in my opinion the key for why they won, despite the bad game four.
